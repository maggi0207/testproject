Affiliates Mapping

In the members array, first look for entries where role = primary.

These are considered the first affiliated members.

After fetching an affiliated member, find the corresponding household member by:

Matching memberID in the same array.

Ensuring role = household.

Ensuring status = current.

Household Mapping

In the members array, first look for entries where role = owner.

After fetching the owner, find the corresponding household member by:

Matching memberID with another item in the same array.

Ensuring role = household.

Ensuring status = current.

Business Mapping

In the members array, look for entries where role = administrator.

If found, that member is considered the business account manager.
